Design Intent

A modern mountain retreat that honours its natural setting through restrained material choices and intentional framing of the surrounding landscape. The design draws the eye outward through floor-to-ceiling glazing while maintaining warmth through natural wood soffits, board-formed concrete, and blackened steel accents.

The interior flow was designed to feel both open and intimate - generous living spaces that compress into quiet private retreats. Light is the defining material, with each room oriented to capture shifting conditions throughout the day.
