Project Brief

Summerhill Backhouse

Measured Architecture • March 13, 2026

Overview

Project Type
Architectural Photography
Location
2847 Summerhill Place, Squamish
Full architectural documentation of completed laneway house. Interior and exterior photography capturing the design intent, material palette, and relationship to the surrounding landscape. Emphasis on natural light, spatial flow, and the connection between indoor and outdoor living.

Deliverables

  • 25-35 edited high-resolution images
  • 5 twilight exterior shots
  • Drone aerial coverage (2 angles)
  • Web-optimized gallery
  • Full licensing for portfolio, awards submissions, and publication

Timeline

Start Date
April 15, 2026
Duration
1 full shoot day + 1 twilight session

Creative Direction

  • Entry approach and street presence
  • Main living area from multiple angles
  • Kitchen details and material closeups
  • Primary bedroom suite
  • Bathroom finishes
  • Staircase and vertical circulation
  • Deck and outdoor living connection
  • Landscape integration
  • Twilight exterior (front and rear)
  • Aerial context shots

Location and Access

Access via lane behind main house. Client will provide gate code day-of. Street parking available on Summerhill Place. No staging required - home is furnished and occupied.

Special Requirements

Coordinate with interior designer (Sarah Chen, Studio North) for any final styling. Homeowner requests morning shoot start to capture east-facing light in primary bedroom.
