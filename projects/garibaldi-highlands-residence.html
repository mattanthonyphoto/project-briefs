Design Intent

A west coast modern residence designed to frame the dramatic views of the Garibaldi Range while creating a sense of shelter and grounding. The home steps down the hillside in three volumes connected by a central spine, with each level opening to the landscape through generous glazing and covered outdoor rooms.

The material palette draws from the surrounding forest — heavy timber structure, natural stone cladding, and weathering steel that will patina over time. The interiors maintain the same honest materiality with exposed Douglas fir beams, polished concrete floors, and custom walnut millwork throughout.
